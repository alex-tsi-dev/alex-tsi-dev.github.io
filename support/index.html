Support and Contact

This page explains how to contact Alex TSI through alex-tsi.dev and how different types of website, privacy, and legal requests are handled.

Last updated: April 8, 2026

1. Main contact channel

The primary contact address for this website is alex.tsi.dev@gmail.com.

You may also use the contact form available on the website for general inquiries, project discussions, and business contact. Messages submitted through the form are routed to a first-party API environment controlled by Alex TSI.

2. General inquiries

Use the main contact email or the website contact form for general questions about the website, published content, or professional background information shown on the site.

3. Project and business inquiries

If you want to discuss a project, collaboration, consulting opportunity, or other professional matter, please include enough detail to understand the nature of your request.

  • Brief description of the project or request.
  • Relevant timeline, scope, or constraints.
  • Your preferred contact details.

4. Privacy and data protection requests

Requests relating to personal data, privacy rights, consent withdrawal, or this website's privacy practices should be sent to alex.tsi.dev@gmail.com.

To help process your request efficiently, please provide enough information to identify the relevant interaction, such as the email address used, approximate date of contact, and a short description of the request.

5. Abuse reports and legal notices

If you need to report abusive use of the contact form, suspected infringement, security concerns, or other formal legal issues, please clearly mark your message and include the relevant URL, date, and supporting information.

6. Response expectations

Alex TSI does not provide a guaranteed response time or 24/7 support service through this website. Messages are handled as reasonably possible based on workload and the nature of the request.

Sending a message does not create any obligation to enter into a contract, provide services, or continue correspondence.

7. Related documents

More information about data processing, cookies, and website use is available in the Privacy Policy, Cookie Notice, and Terms of Use. Public operator and website identification details are listed in the Legal Notice.
