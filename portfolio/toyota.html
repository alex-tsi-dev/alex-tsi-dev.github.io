Car sales management platform for Toyota dealership centers

The project aimed to simplify and enhance the car sales process for dealership centers, providing a user-friendly platform for managing inventory, customer interactions, and sales transactions. The system streamlined operations, improved efficiency, and offered better customer engagement, ultimately helping dealerships sell vehicles more effectively.

As a .NET tech lead, I made tech decisions, managed CI/CD pipelines, automated deployments, and implemented comprehensive logging and monitoring solutions. The platform was built using ASP.NET MVC with Azure DevOps for continuous integration and deployment, ensuring reliable and efficient operations.
