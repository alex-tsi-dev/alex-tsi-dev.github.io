Tracking patient history and document flow

As a key contributor to a significant project, I played a vital role in enhancing the seamless tracking of patient history and document flow within American public health institutions. The project, featuring core modules such as patient profile editing, procedure monitoring, and medical device tracking, was extensive and had been developed over an extended period. Navigating this large-scale system was both challenging and rewarding. The codebase was substantial, and managing legacy components presented its own complexities. Nevertheless, my responsibilities in developing new features and providing support were pivotal to the ongoing success of the project. Despite the challenges, I found fulfillment in the continuous improvement and optimization of this healthcare platform.
