Modern automotive service website for a trusted car studio

Ghost Garage is a comprehensive automotive service website created for a car studio specializing in windshield replacement, glass polishing, chip repair, and PPF protection. The platform features a modern and fully responsive design that provides clear navigation, structured service information, and accessible contact details, helping customers quickly find what they need.

The website is also enhanced with a built-in ChatBot Assistant that allows users to describe their issue in a convenient way, and contact the studio effortlessly. This smart functionality improves communication, streamlines the service process, and adds an innovative touch that elevates the overall user experience.
