Cookie Notice

This Cookie Notice explains how cookies and similar technologies may be used on the Alex TSI website.

Last updated: April 8, 2026

1. What this notice covers

This notice applies to cookies, pixels, local storage entries, browser identifiers, and similar technologies used in connection with this website, including analytics and performance monitoring tools.

2. What cookies and similar technologies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies may also store or read information from your browser or device, or may rely on unique identifiers generated through browser interactions.

3. Categories of technologies used on this website

The website may use the following categories of technologies:

  • Strictly necessary technologies: used for core website operation, security, traffic management, consent storage, and reliable delivery of website functions.
  • Analytics technologies: used to understand how visitors use the website, which pages are viewed, how users navigate, and how content performs. For this website, that includes Google Analytics 4.
  • Performance and diagnostics technologies: used to measure availability, investigate technical errors, analyze browser-side performance, and maintain service reliability. For this website, that includes New Relic browser monitoring. Server-side APM may also process request and performance data without storing cookies on your device.

4. Consent model

This website is intended to use a consent-first model for non-essential technologies. Analytics and browser monitoring technologies should not be activated until the relevant consent choice has been made through the website's consent mechanism.

Strictly necessary technologies may still be used without prior consent where permitted by applicable law because they support core website operation, security, or storage of your consent choice.

5. How these technologies may be used

The website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • remember your consent choices;
  • maintain secure and stable website operation;
  • measure website traffic and engagement trends;
  • identify broken pages, performance issues, and browser errors;
  • improve content, navigation, and technical reliability.

6. Managing your preferences

You can manage your preferences through the website's consent banner or equivalent consent control made available on the website. You may also restrict or delete cookies through your browser settings, although doing so may affect certain website functions.

If you withdraw consent, non-essential analytics and browser monitoring should stop loading for future interactions once your updated choice has been applied.

7. Third-party processing and international access

Analytics and monitoring technologies may involve third-party service providers that process device, usage, diagnostics, or performance information. Some of these providers or their sub-processors may operate outside the European Economic Area. Where required, appropriate safeguards are intended to be used under applicable law.

8. Technical details

The exact technical names, storage periods, and implementation details of cookies or similar identifiers may change over time as the website, analytics setup, consent settings, and monitoring tools are updated.

This notice describes the categories and purposes of technologies used on the website. It does not promise that a specific cookie name, lifespan, or provider configuration will remain unchanged.

For more information about how personal data is processed, see the Privacy Policy. Questions about this notice may be sent to alex.tsi.dev@gmail.com.
