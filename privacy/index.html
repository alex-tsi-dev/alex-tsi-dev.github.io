Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is processed in connection with alex-tsi.dev, including website browsing, the contact form, consent management, analytics, monitoring, and related support communications.

Last updated: April 11, 2026

1. Who operates this website

This website is operated by Alex TSI, a sole proprietor established in Poland. For the purposes of applicable data protection law, including the GDPR where applicable, Alex TSI acts as the controller of personal data processed through this website.

Detailed website operator and trader identification for alex-tsi.dev is provided in the Legal Notice.

2. Scope of this policy

This policy applies to personal data processed when you browse the website, interact with its content, use the contact form, communicate by email, store or update consent preferences, or allow analytics and monitoring technologies used on the website.

3. Personal data collected through the website

The data collected depends on how you interact with the website. It may include:

  • Your name, email address, and message content when you use the contact form.
  • Information you include in direct emails sent to the published contact address.
  • Routine technical data such as IP address, browser type, device information, timestamps, requested pages, referrer data, and similar request or security log information.
  • Usage, performance, diagnostics, and error data collected through analytics and monitoring tools when you consent to non-essential tracking.

Please do not submit special category data, payment data, or confidential information that is not necessary for your inquiry.

4. How personal data is collected

Personal data may be collected directly from you, automatically when you use the website, and from the website's technical infrastructure.

When you use the contact form, your message is sent to a first-party API environment controlled by Alex TSI. Contact submissions may include the details you type into the form, plus technical delivery and security metadata such as IP address, browser details, request timing, and server-side processing records needed to operate the contact workflow.

The website also stores a first-party consent record so your privacy choices can be remembered. Google Analytics 4 and New Relic browser monitoring load only after the relevant consent choice has been made through the website's consent mechanism, beginning with the first-load consent message and its Accept action.

If remote front-end asset delivery remains enabled in production, standard browser requests may also be made to Google font delivery services and Cloudflare's cdnjs service when pages load.

5. Purposes of processing

Personal data may be used to:

  • Operate, host, secure, and maintain the website.
  • Receive, review, and respond to contact requests and business inquiries.
  • Take steps requested by you before a possible business engagement.
  • Understand website usage, improve content, and measure audience interaction.
  • Monitor website availability, performance, and errors.
  • Prevent abuse, misuse, and security incidents.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect legal claims.

6. Legal bases for processing

Depending on the context, personal data may be processed on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Legitimate interests, including website operation, security, troubleshooting, abuse prevention, and handling ordinary communications.
  • Steps taken at your request prior to entering into a contract, where your inquiry relates to potential services, collaboration, or a business opportunity.
  • Consent, where non-essential analytics, browser monitoring, cookies, or similar technologies are used.
  • Compliance with legal obligations where applicable.

7. Cookies, analytics, and monitoring

The website uses cookies and similar technologies as described in the Cookie Policy.

For this website, Google Analytics 4 and New Relic browser monitoring are treated as non-essential technologies. They load only after the relevant consent choice has been made through the website's consent mechanism.

The website also stores a first-party consent preference record using the storage key alexTsiConsent.v1 so your selected categories can be remembered across visits. On first load, the website records consent through the simplified consent message and its Accept button. You can later reopen and update these settings at any time through the Cookie Settings control shown in the website footer.

New Relic APM is also used on the server side to monitor application performance and reliability. Some server-side monitoring functions may not rely on cookies stored on your device.

8. Recipients and categories of processors

Personal data may be shared, as necessary, with the following categories of recipients and named providers that support this website:

  • Hosting and infrastructure providers that host alex-tsi.dev and the first-party contact API environment.
  • Google services used for Google Analytics 4 and related script delivery, subject to analytics consent.
  • New Relic, Inc. for browser monitoring and optional application performance monitoring, subject to monitoring consent.
  • Google font delivery services where remote Google Fonts requests remain enabled.
  • Cloudflare's cdnjs service where remote icon or stylesheet delivery remains enabled.
  • Email and communications service providers used to receive, route, or respond to inquiries.
  • Professional advisers, service providers, and contractors who support website operations or legal compliance.
  • Courts, regulators, authorities, or law enforcement where disclosure is required or reasonably necessary.

The website does not use a public third-party contact form platform for the form submission itself. The contact workflow is routed through a first-party API controlled by Alex TSI, although supporting infrastructure or email services may still process related data.

9. International transfers

Some of the providers listed above, especially global analytics, monitoring, CDN, font delivery, and infrastructure services, may process personal data outside the European Economic Area.

Where that happens, Alex TSI relies on safeguards recognized by applicable law for the relevant provider and transfer context, such as an adequacy decision, the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework where applicable, or standard contractual clauses with supplementary measures where required.

10. Retention

Personal data is kept only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above and in line with the website's published retention rules.

  • Contact form submissions and related correspondence are intended to be retained for 12 months after the last substantive interaction, unless a longer period is needed to follow up on the request or protect legal claims.
  • Routine server, request, and security logs are intended to be retained for 30 days, unless a longer period is required for incident handling, abuse prevention, or legal obligations.
  • Google Analytics 4 data is retained for 14 months.
  • New Relic browser telemetry is retained for 30 days.

11. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to request access to your personal data, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, objection to processing, and data portability. Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal.

To exercise your rights, contact alex.tsi.dev@gmail.com. Reasonable verification may be required before a request is fulfilled.

12. Complaints

If you believe your data has been handled unlawfully, you may contact Alex TSI first to seek a practical resolution. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the President of the Personal Data Protection Office in Poland (Prezes Urzedu Ochrony Danych Osobowych, UODO) or another competent supervisory authority, where applicable.

13. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the website, legal requirements, or operational practices. The updated version will be published on this page with a revised effective date.

14. Contact

Privacy questions and data requests may be sent to alex.tsi.dev@gmail.com. General operator details are also listed in the Legal Notice.
